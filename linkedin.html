About us

Lifelancer is an AI-powered platform for talent hiring in Life Sciences, Pharma, Healthtech & IT. The platform connects employers with local, freelance, remote & international talent in the above domains. You can find jobs in pharma, biotech, health sciences, healthtech, & IT domains on our platform, as well as, you can quickly connect with top talent & 50,000+ experts on Lifelancer. In addition, Lifelancer facilitates international hiring compliance and payroll services in collaboration with market-leading partners. We are on a mission to empower life science talent as well as life science companies & organizations.
